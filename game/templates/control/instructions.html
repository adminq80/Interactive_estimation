Game instructions

After completing the instructions and comprehension check, you will begin the game. The game consists of 20 rounds. At each round you will see a scatter plot and be asked to estimate the correlation of the X and Y variables.
Correlation measures how closely two variables are related to each other. For example, the graph below depicts the correlation between age (the X variable), and height (the Y variable). As age increases, height tends to increase as well.

Next

In this game, your correlation guess can range from 0 to 1, and the closer the points fit to a straight line, the closer the correlation will be to 1. No calculations are necessary, just make your best estimate.
