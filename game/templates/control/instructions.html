Game Instructions


After completing the instructions and comprehension check, you will begin the game. The game consists of 20 rounds. At each round you will see a scatter plot and be asked to estimate the correlation of the X and Y variables.
Correlation measures how strongly two variables are related to each other. For example, the graph below depicts the correlation between weight and height. As weight increases, height tends to increase as well.

Game Instructions

Each round will consist of 2 stages: "Your Guess" and "Round Outcome".

1. Your Guess: Guess the correlation of the plot shown. Use the slider below the plot to pick a value, and click "Submit" when you are ready. You can take maximum 30 seconds to submit.
