Game Instructions


After completing the instructions and comprehension check, you will enter the game's lobby. The game will start automatically after 12 players enter the lobby, and you will hear a bell ring.
The game consists of 20 rounds. At each round you will see a scatter plot and be asked to estimate the correlation of the X and Y variables. Correlation measures how strongly two variables are related to each other. For example, the graph below depicts the correlation between age and height. As age increases, height tends to increase as well.

Game Instructions

In this game, your correlation guess can range from 0 to 1, and the closer the points fit to a straight line, the closer the correlation will be to 1. No calculations are necessary, just make your best estimate.

Game Instructions

Each round will consist of 3 stages: "Your Guess", "Interactive Guess", and "Round Outcome".

1. Your Guess: Guess the correlation of the plot shown. Use the slider below the plot to pick a value, and click "Submit" when you are ready. You can take maximum 30 seconds to submit.

2. Interactive Guess: Here you will see the guesses of other players. You can change your guess by using the slider bar, and see how other players change their guesses in real-time. Click "Submit" when you are ready to submit your final guess. You can take maximum 30 seconds to submit.
