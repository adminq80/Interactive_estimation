Game instructions

{#Edit text#}

After completing the instructions and comprehension check, you will enter the lobby. The game will start automatically after 12 players enter the lobby, and you will hear a bell ring.
The game consists of 20 rounds. At each round you will see a scatter plot and be asked to estimate the correlation of the X and Y variables. Correlation measures how closely two variables are related to each other. For example, the graph below depicts the correlation between age and height. As age increases, height tends to increase as well.

Next {# #}
In this game, your correlation guess can range from 0 to 1, and the closer the points fit to a straight line, the closer the correlation will be to 1. No calculations are necessary, just make your best estimate.
Previous Next

Note that many different graphs may have the same correlation value. All 4 of the graphs below have a correlation of 0.82.

Previous Next

Each round will consist of 3 stages: "Your Guess", "Interactive Guess", and "Round Outcome".

1. Your Guess: Guess the correlation of the plot shown. Use the slider below the plot to pick a value, and click "Submit" when you are ready. You can take maximum 30 seconds to submit.

Previous Next
2. Interactive Guess: In this stage you will see the guesses of other players. You can change your guess by using the slider bar, and see how other players change theirs in real-time. Click "Submit" when you are ready to submit your final guess. You can take maximum 30 seconds to submit.
Previous Next
3. Round Overview: See your final guess vs. the actual correlation value, and the score you earned.
See the scores of all players, and choose who you want to follow for then next round. Use the unfollow and follow buttons to choose a maximum of 3 players.
Click “Next” to go to the next round.
